The Meaning Behind the Name

Kimaya — Divine Alchemy

In ancient Sanskrit, Kimaya means divine alchemy — the sacred art of transformation, where the ordinary becomes extraordinary through intention, mastery, and a touch of magic.

At Kimaya's Kitchen, we honour this meaning in every dish we create. Our restaurant was born from a singular vision: to transform the rich, time-honoured recipes of India through the lens of modern culinary innovation — preserving the soul of tradition while reimagining its expression.

Founded in 2018, Kimaya's Kitchen began as an intimate supper club where Chef Arjun Malhotra would invite guests into a world where heritage spices met avant-garde techniques, where the warmth of a family kitchen embraced the precision of fine dining. What started as a whispered secret among food lovers has blossomed into a destination celebrated for its daring, its beauty, and its reverence for the craft.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries within it a story — of grandmothers who ground spices by hand at dawn, of bustling markets fragrant with cardamom and saffron, and of a new generation of chefs who dare to dream in flavour. This is the magic of Kimaya: tradition, transformed.
