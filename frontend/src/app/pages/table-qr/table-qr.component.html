How to Use Table QR Codes

01

Generate

Enter your restaurant's menu URL above and click "Generate QR Code" to create a unique QR code for your tables.

02

Print

Use the print button to print QR codes on high-quality card stock. Consider laminating them for durability.

03

Place

Position QR codes on each table using elegant table stands or embed them into table tents and menu holders.

04

Delight

Guests scan the QR code with their phone camera to instantly access your full digital menu — no app required.

Pro Tips

  • Print at a minimum size of 2×2 inches for reliable scanning from arm's length.
  • Ensure good contrast — dark QR codes on a light background work best.
  • Test every printed QR code with multiple phone models before placing on tables.
  • Update the URL if your menu page changes, then reprint the QR codes.
  • Consider adding your restaurant logo near the QR code for brand consistency.
